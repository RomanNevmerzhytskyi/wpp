NEWS

21 November 2023

Zalgiris parts ways with Mitrou-Long by mutual agreement

Zalgiris Kaunas says goodbye to defender Naz Mitrou-Long. The Lithuanian champions terminated the contract with the 30-year-old Canadian by mutual agreement.

19 November 2023

Zalgiris takes home victory over Siauliai

Zalgiris Kaunas (9-1) won their ninth victory in this season’s Betsafe-LKL Championship. Kazys Maksvytis’ team defeated Siauliai (1-9) by 89-74 (16-15, 29-20, 22-28, 22-11) at Zalgirio Arena.

17 November 2023

Zalgiris breaks the spell on the last shot against Bayern

It came down to the final shot, but Zalgiris Kaunas ultimately outlasted a feisty Bayern Munich for a 74-73 victory in Round 9 of the 2023-24 Turkish Airlines EuroLeague Regular Season.

15 November 2023

Zalgiris loses to Panathinaikos in the final seconds

Zalgiris Kaunas (3-5) started the EuroLeague double week with a defeat in Greece. Kazys Maksvytis’ team lost 71-73 (15-13, 24-23, 13-17, 19-20) to Panathinaikos Athens (4-4) on the road.

12 November 2023

Zalgiris suffers its first local defeat in Vilnius

Zalgiris Kaunas (8-1) suffered their first defeat in the Betsafe-LKL Championship. Lithuanian champions lost 64-75 (14-17, 19-13, 12-22, 19-23) to Wolves Vilnius (8-1) on the road.

9 November 2023

Zalgiris erases 18-point deficit, but loses in overtime against Anadolu Efes

Zalgiris Kaunas made it to overtime in Round 7 of the 2023-24 Turkish Airlines EuroLeague Regular Season, but eventually lost 82-86 to Anadolu Efes Istanbul on the road.
